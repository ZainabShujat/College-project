All Tested Medicines and Their Description

11. DEXTROMETHORPHAN

What is it?
DEXTROMETHORPHAN is a cough suppressant used to relieve coughing caused by common colds, flu, or respiratory infections.

Common Brands in India:
- VICKS: Known for its effectiveness in treating cough and cold symptoms.
- ROBUSTUSS: A widely available cough syrup.
- BENADRYL COUGH: A common cough syrup brand that helps with both cough and cold symptoms.

Ranking by Quality:
- Vicks
- Robustuss
- Benadryl Cough

12. GUAIFENESIN

What is it?
GUAIFENESIN is an expectorant that helps thin and loosen mucus in the airways, making it easier to cough up.

Common Brands in India:
- CHERICOUGH: One of the most popular expectorants.
- BENADRYL COUGH PLUS: Combines Guaifenesin and an antihistamine.
- RUBITUSS: Another effective option for treating cough.

Ranking by Quality:
- Chericough
- Benadryl Cough Plus
- Rubituss

13. AMBROXOL

What is it?
AMBROXOL is a mucolytic agent used to break down and thin mucus in the airways.

Common Brands in India:
- AMBROLITE: A trusted mucolytic brand.
- MUCOLITE: Used for treating respiratory conditions.
- AMBROGEL: Popular in both liquid and tablet form.

Ranking by Quality:
- Ambrolite
- Mucolite
- Ambrogel

14. CALAMINE

What is it?
CALAMINE is used to relieve itching and skin irritation caused by insect bites, rashes, and poison ivy.

Common Brands in India:
- CALADRYL: Effective for soothing irritated skin.
- OXYCAL: Provides gentle relief for skin conditions.
- CALAMINE LOTION (Generic): The affordable alternative for treating minor skin irritations.

Ranking by Quality:
- Caladryl
- Oxical
- Calamine Lotion (Generic)

15. LOPERAMIDE

What is it?
LOPERAMIDE is an anti-diarrheal medication used to reduce the frequency of diarrhea by slowing down the movement of the muscles in the intestines.

Common Brands in India:
- IMODIUM: A widely used brand for treating diarrhea.
- LOPAMID: A cost-effective option.
- DIAROL: An affordable alternative for mild to moderate diarrhea.

Ranking by Quality:
- Imodium
- Lopamid
- Diarol
