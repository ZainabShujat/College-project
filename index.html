All Tested Medicines and Their Description

1. PARACETAMOL

What is it?
PARACETAMOL, also known as acetaminophen, is a commonly used pain reliever and fever reducer. It is used for mild to moderate pain relief, such as headaches, muscle aches, and minor arthritis.

Common Brands in India:
- DOLONEX: A popular paracetamol-based product for fever and pain relief.
- PARACET: A widely used brand for headaches and fever.
- CALPOL: A trusted pediatric brand commonly used to treat children.
- TYLENOL: A well-known brand worldwide for pain and fever relief.
- DISPRIN: An effervescent tablet form of paracetamol for faster relief.

Ranking by Quality:
- Dolonex
- Paracet
- Calpol
- Tylenol
- Disprin

2. IBUPROFEN

What is it?
IBUPROFEN is a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) used for reducing inflammation, pain, and fever. It is effective for treating conditions like arthritis, menstrual cramps, and muscle injuries.

Common Brands in India:
- BRUFEN: One of the most trusted ibuprofen brands for treating pain and inflammation.
- IBUGESIC: A popular choice for pain relief and fever reduction.
- ADVIL: A globally recognized brand for pain relief and reducing fever.
- MEFTAL: Commonly used for muscle pain and menstrual cramps.
- IBUPROGEL: A topical gel variant of ibuprofen for localized pain relief.

Ranking by Quality:
- Brufen
- Ibugesic
- Advil
- Meftal
- Ibuprogell

3. LORATADINE

What is it?
LORATADINE is an antihistamine used to treat allergies like hay fever, rhinitis, and hives. It provides relief from symptoms like sneezing, runny nose, and itchy or watery eyes.

Common Brands in India:
- CLARITIN: A well-known antihistamine brand that offers effective relief from allergies.
- LOREZ: A generic version of loratadine, commonly prescribed for allergy symptoms.
- ALERID: Popular for treating allergy symptoms with a fast-acting formula.
- FEXODAY: Another antihistamine that works similarly to loratadine for hay fever and other allergies.

Ranking by Quality:
- Claritin
- Lorez
- Alerid
- Fexoday

4. CETIRIZINE

What is it?
CETIRIZINE is an antihistamine used to relieve allergy symptoms such as runny nose, sneezing, itchy eyes, and itching caused by hives. It is widely used for treating both seasonal and perennial allergies.

Common Brands in India:
- ZYRTEC: A leading brand for treating allergy symptoms effectively.
- CETRIZ: A generic alternative with the same active ingredient as Zyrtec.
- ALERGON: Used to treat seasonal and perennial allergies with minimal sedative effects.
- CLARIDINE: Known for its effectiveness in treating skin allergies and rhinitis.

Ranking by Quality:
- Zyrtec
- Cetriz
- Alergon
- Claridine

5. DIPHENHYDRAMINE

What is it?
DIPHENHYDRAMINE is an antihistamine commonly used for treating allergic reactions, insomnia, and motion sickness. It works by blocking histamine in the body, reducing allergy symptoms and inducing sleep.

Common Brands in India:
- BENADRYL: The most recognized brand for treating both allergy symptoms and promoting sleep.
- UNISOM: Primarily used for insomnia and sleep disturbances caused by allergies.
- DIAMOD: Used for treating allergic reactions and motion sickness.

Ranking by Quality:
- Benadryl
- Unisom
- Diamod
