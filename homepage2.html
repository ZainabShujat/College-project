All Tested Medicines and Their Description

6. ANTACIDS (Calcium Carbonate)

What is it?
ANTACIDS, specifically calcium carbonate, neutralize stomach acid and provide relief from heartburn and acid reflux. These are commonly used for managing symptoms of indigestion and GERD.

Common Brands in India:
- ENO: One of the most popular antacid brands for instant relief from acidity and indigestion.
- ROLAIDS: Known for its effectiveness in reducing heartburn and acid reflux.
- OVALTINE ANTACID: Provides relief from gastric problems and helps balance the pH in the stomach.
- PRILOSEC: A trusted option for treating GERD and acid reflux.

Ranking by Quality:
- Eno
- Rolaids
- Ovaltine Antacid
- Prilosec

7. FEXOFENADINE

What is it?
FEXOFENADINE is an antihistamine used for treating seasonal allergies and hay fever. It helps relieve symptoms such as sneezing, runny nose, and itchy or watery eyes.

Common Brands in India:
- ALLEGRA: A highly trusted brand for effective allergy relief.
- FEXO: A generic version of fexofenadine that is equally effective for treating hay fever and other allergies.

Ranking by Quality:
- Allegra
- Fexo

8. PSEUDOEPHEDRINE

What is it?
PSEUDOEPHEDRINE is a decongestant used to relieve nasal congestion caused by colds, sinus infections, or allergies. It works by shrinking the blood vessels in the nasal passages to reduce swelling and congestion.

Common Brands in India:
- SUDAFED: A leading brand for relieving nasal congestion and improving airflow.
- PSEUDOFED: A widely available generic decongestant with the same active ingredient.

Ranking by Quality:
- Sudafed
- Pseudofed

9. ASPIRIN

What is it?
ASPIRIN is a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) used to reduce pain, fever, and inflammation. It is commonly used for headache relief, muscle pain, and to prevent heart attacks and strokes.

Common Brands in India:
- DISPRIN: A popular effervescent tablet form for quicker relief from pain and fever.
- ECOTRIN: A branded aspirin used primarily for its cardiovascular benefits.
- ASPRO: A common aspirin brand for pain relief and anti-inflammatory purposes.

Ranking by Quality:
- Disprin
- Ecotrin
- Aspro

10. AMMONIUM CHLORIDE

What is it?
AMMONIUM CHLORIDE is used to treat coughs and colds by acting as an expectorant, helping to loosen mucus and clear the airways.

Common Brands in India:
- AMBROXOL: Often combined with other ingredients for enhanced effectiveness in treating coughs.
- AMMONIACAL: Used for respiratory conditions and to ease chest congestion.

Ranking by Quality:
- Ambroxol
- Ammoniacal
